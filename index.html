The Villa

An Extraordinary Home in the Heart of Marrakech

Nestled within the prestigious Amelkis Golf Resort, Villa Butterfly is a masterpiece of contemporary Moroccan architecture. Its architect-designed interiors blend traditional craftsmanship with modern luxury, creating a space that is both breathtakingly beautiful and deeply comfortable.

Accommodating up to 12 guests across 4 sumptuous private suites, the villa boasts panoramic Atlas Mountain views, a 14×5m swimming pool, a traditional hammam, massage room, private gym, multiple indoor lounges, a fully equipped kitchen, rooftop terrace, and lush garden with outdoor dining.

  • 14×5m private pool — signature yellow tiles (depth 1.2–2.05m)
  • Traditional Moroccan hammam & private massage room
  • Private gym, rooftop terrace & outdoor lounge
  • Panoramic views of Atlas Mountains & Amelkis Golf
  • Just 15 minutes from the Marrakech Medina
